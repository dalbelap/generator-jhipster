Cookies policy

We use cookies to facilitate the use of our website. Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on the hard drive of your computer and you are required to use our site. We use them to save the User Session once identified, to better understand the way in which you use our website and thus consequently to improve the navigation process.

Cookies are the benchmark that indicates, for example, if a page of our website has been seen before, or if your visit is new or recurrent. The cookies we use do not store personal data any, or any information that could identify. If you do not want to receive cookies, please configure your Internet browser to delete them from the hard drive of your computer, block them or warn you in case of installation of the same.
